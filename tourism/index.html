Queen of hills (Nilgiris) is always top on the go-to list in weekend drive for fellow Coimbatorean's. Just a 84km away from coimbatore, which is home for Nilgiri tahr (State animal of Tamilnadu) this district has plenty of sightseeing places and other tourist attractions starting from the only rack railway in India scaling an elevation of 326 m to 2,203 m which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site under the name of "Mountain Railways of India", Mudumalai national park, Doddabetta peak (highest peak in south India), Pykara waterfalls, Ooty lake, Bison valley and Avalanche. Perur Pateeswarar Temple, which is situated in western part of coimbatore and it's believed to be one of the places where lord Shiva have performed Ananda Thandavam (one of two dance forms associated with Lord Shiva). One of the ancient temples in state which was built in 2nd century by great king Karikala Chola of chola dynasty in dravidian architecture style which stands still. This Temple deity was worshipped by "PATTI" the daughter of the holy cow "Kamdhenu". Hence the name Pateeswarar.

Starts with one of the important mechanical invention "The wheel of Mesapotamia", this museum carries the history of the evolution of wheels from ancient period to our modern world. Starting from the model of first car Benz Patent-Motorwagen manufactured in 1886 to the modern era sports cars this museum houses only 1140 units manufactured Morris Cowley Bullnose, Hispano-Suiza, Cadillac limousine etc. which we don't see in our day to day life. A visit to this museum will really help you learn the evolution of cars and how it operates. All those antique mechanical and engineering marvels made as car by British, German, French, American, Japanese, Spanish and Indian manufacturers.

One of Adrenaline Pumping Activities in the city is the tough trek to Velliangiri hills (called as kailash of south) which will be a fun and test for your mental & physical strength, consists of 7 mountains with 13 kms distance (two-way), ends with the worship of Swayambhu lingha (created by its own accord) which lies in the seventh hill and you will start to feel the embrace of cloud and in sometime you will be on top of the clouds which is 6000 feet altitude.
